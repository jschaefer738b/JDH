THE CAMP.

SUNNINGDALE.
My dear Dyer
I received your letter dated
21 only yesterday.
By all means send Harriets
drawings over to the Herb., where I
hope to be on Monday, & I will
get them published as soon as
possible : I fear it cannot be before
July Number at earliest : to get all
in one Number.
I am extremely sorry that the
Ceylon flora is causing you so
much trouble. Dulau's conduct is
inexplicable.
